Blackrock Athletic Club


About Us

Blackrock A.C. is located in the seaside village of Blackrock, Co. Louth, Ireland. We cater for both juvenile and senior athletes in the age range from six and up. We have over 200 active members in our club. These athletes take part in cross country running, track running, and various field events, such as long jump, javelin and shot put. We train on the Community Field and in the Community Centre in Blackrock.

New members are always welcome to join our club. To join us, bring a completed membership application form along to any training session.

Training Times

Blackrock Athletic Club train on Blackrock Community Field and in Blackrock Community Centre. Training times are 6-7pm on Mondays and Thursdays (excluding bank holidays). For insurance purposes, all 6 year old children must be accompanied by an adult or guardian for the entire duration of each training session that they attend.

To join Blackrock Athletic Club, print the membership application form and bring the completed form along to any training session.
