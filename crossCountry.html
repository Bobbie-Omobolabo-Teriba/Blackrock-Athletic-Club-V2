Blackrock Athletic Club



Cross Country

Blackrock A.C. is hosting its annual Cross Country race meeting on Sunday, 31st January 2016.

The event will take place at the Geraldines Gaelic Football Club in Haggardstown.

First race is at 12 noon

Races include:

Girls

U7 - 100m

U9 - 500m

U11 - 1000m

U13 - 1500m

U15 - 2000m

U17 - 2500m

U19 - 2500m

Fit-for-Life - 2500m

Boys

U7 - 100m

U9 - 500m

U11 - 1000m

U13 - 1500m

U15 - 2000m

U17 - 2500m

U19 - 2500m

Fit-for-Life - 2500m

Medals will be given to the first three finishers in each race.

Medals will be given to the first three teams (three athletes per team) in each race.

An entrance fee of €2 per child and €3 per adult will be charged at the gate.

Further details can be got by contacting Mary Scanlon @ 086-4081549

This event is being held in accordance with A.A.I. rules.

In particular, on the 31st December 2016, athletes must still be younger than the age group that they are competing in.
