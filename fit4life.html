Fit 4 Life

To join the club as a adult member, please complete and return the attached application form.

Training will commence on Monday 8th April 6-7pm. Please ensure you have a completed application form and your 40 euro membership fee with you. Please also ensure you have appropriate walking/running shoes and clothing.

Thereafter training will be at the following times:

Mondays 6-7pm

Thursdays 6-7pm

Sundays 10.30-11.30am

There is also the option of an additional run on Wednesdays at 9.15am if there is sufficient interest. Details will be posted on the Facebook group www.facebook.com/groups/BlackrockFit4Life

We look forward to training with you soon.
